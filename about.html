🧭 Who We Are

Discover the story behind Nepal's most trusted event management company with over 15 years of excellence in creating unforgettable celebrations.

Sreenagar Events

Sreenagar Events is a full-service event management and rental service provider based in Bhairahawa, Nepal. Formerly known as Shreenagar Tent House, we have over 15 years of expertise in organizing a diverse range of Nepali events, from intimate family functions to large-scale celebrations with over 1,500 guests.

Whether you're planning a grand wedding, bratabanda, birthday bash, religious ceremony, corporate event, or even a product launch—our dedicated team brings your vision to life with precision, passion, and creativity.

We blend traditional Nepali elements with modern event solutions to deliver seamless, beautiful, and unforgettable experiences that reflect the rich cultural heritage of Nepal while meeting contemporary expectations.
