Why Choose Sreenagar Events?

Nepal's Most Trusted Event Partner

Sreenagar Events is a full-service event management and rental service provider based in Bhairahawa, Nepal. Formerly known as Shreenagar Tent House, we have over 15 years of expertise in organizing diverse Nepali events.

We blend traditional Nepali elements with modern event solutions to deliver seamless, beautiful, and unforgettable experiences for celebrations ranging from intimate family functions to grand festivals with over 1,500 guests.
