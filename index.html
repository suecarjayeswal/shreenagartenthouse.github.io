Our Event Services.

With over 15 years of experience, Shreenagar Tent House offers a complete range of event management and rental services in Bhairahawa and across Nepal. Whether you are planning an intimate family function or a grand celebration, our team ensures every detail is handled with care and professionalism.

Event Planning & Coordination

From weddings and bratabanda to birthdays and corporate events, we provide end-to-end planning, vendor management, and on-site coordination for a seamless experience.

Tent & Canopy Rentals

High-quality tents, canopies, and marquees for all event sizes and styles. Our setups are sturdy, weather-resistant, and beautifully decorated to match your theme.

Stage, Lighting & Sound

Professional stage design, vibrant lighting, and crystal-clear sound systems for ceremonies, performances, and speeches. We ensure your event looks and sounds spectacular.

Event Décor & Theming

Customized décor solutions including floral arrangements, draping, centerpieces, and themed props to transform your venue into a memorable setting.

Catering & Hospitality

Delicious catering options and trained service staff to delight your guests. We handle everything from snacks to full-course meals for any occasion.

Furniture & Equipment Rental

Comfortable chairs, tables, sofas, and essential event equipment available for rent. Our inventory suits both small gatherings and large festivals.

Who Are We.

Shreenagar Tent House is a premier event management and rental service provider based in Bhairahawa, Nepal. With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in organizing a wide range of Nepali events including weddings, bratabanda, engagements, receptions, birthdays, and large religious gatherings like Mahayagya. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and personalized service has made us a trusted partner for clients across Nepal.

Our Process.

We begin by understanding your vision and requirements, followed by meticulous planning and coordination. Our team manages every detail from vendor selection to on-site execution, ensuring a seamless and memorable event.

Our Approach.

We believe in personalized service, cultural sensitivity, and attention to detail. Our approach combines traditional Nepali event customs with modern event management techniques to deliver exceptional experiences.

Our Goal.

Our goal is to make every event unique and unforgettable, providing professional support that allows our clients to enjoy their special occasions without stress.

Our Mission.

To be the leading event management and rental service in Nepal, known for excellence, reliability, and heartfelt service.
